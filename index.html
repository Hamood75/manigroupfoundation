About Mani Group Foundation

Indeed, Allah Helps Those Who Help Themselves

At Mani Group Foundation, we are dedicated to uplifting communities through Islamic values of charity, compassion, and service. Our mission is to provide essential aid, education, and sustainable solutions to those in need, empowering them to build a better future.

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, has access to basic needs, education, and opportunities, inspired by Islamic teachings of charity (Sadaqah) and social justice.

Our Mission

To serve humanity through the principles of Islam by providing essential aid, education, and sustainable development, fostering a just and compassionate society guided by the values of mercy, generosity, and solidarity.
